Inspector Jacques Clouseu
The best french detective that ever lived.

Born in 1965 to a family tree which spans multitude generations of exceptional police officers.
Right from start he already exhibitted natural talent for his line of work. When he was just 2 months old his first words were: You're under arrest!
Proud parents noticing young one natural talent send him to most prestigous police academies at the tender age of 8, as a result Jacques became an expert in international statues of law.
He was awarded with French Medal of Honor after he successfuly recovered national treasure, the pink diamond dubbed: 'The Pink Panther' that has been stolen by a criminal.
He is most famous for his unorthodox methods of interrogation notably: 'good cop bad cop routine' that he performed single handedly and exceptional camouflage skills.
Inspector Jacques can speak Mandarin, French and English fluently however he has had some problems with the latter.
